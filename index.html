Steps to Follow

1

Design Your Website:

  • Create an organized and visually appealing layout.
  • Consider user-friendly navigation and responsive design for various devices.
2

Create Essential Pages:

  • Include pages like Home, About, Courses, Contact.
  • Provide comprehensive information about your course offerings.
3

Responsiveness:

  • Merge all your pages and link them with each other.
  • Make your website responsive.
4

Testing:

  • Thoroughly test your website's functionality, usability, and responsiveness across different devices and browsers.
